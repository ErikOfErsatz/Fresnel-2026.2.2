FREE RESOURCE for EDITING STARS, NABBED-ITEMS, ENERGY, & LOCATIONS

This is a tool for modifying your save data for Eternity Egg. Its useful for:

-Fixing softlocks, like being stuck Out Of Bounds
-Testing quests without grinding through the game each time
-Testing new locations/items/quests which are implemented but otherwise inaccessible (e.g. Rootrot Salve)
